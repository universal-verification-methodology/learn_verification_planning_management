# Welcome to verification planning

Welcome to verification planning and management

---

## What you’ll build toward
- The arc is plan, coverage, regression, sign-off
- VIP handoff deliverables
- Deep VIP engines stay in protocol and UVM courses

---

## Two tracks, one idea
- Track A is planning docs you keep
- Track B is the platform’s verification-planning boards
- You may do either track, or both
- A good rhythm is browser board first for vocabulary

---

## Set up Track A
- Open this course folder and skim the module READMEs
- Keep a notebook or markdown file ready for small tables
- Optional: browse the legacy planning materials for templates
- From the course root, self-check scripts can grade checklist items when you want them
- Helpful prereq

---

- From the monorepo
- All twelve planning labs ship, from plan checklist through VIP handoff
- If you prefer, use the published tools site instead
