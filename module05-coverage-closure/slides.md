# Coverage closure

Finding holes is not closing them

---

## Hole plus next-test idea
- A good idea names how you will hit the hole
- Generic “more random” is weak when the hole needs a directed hit
- Mismatch means the idea targets the wrong hole
- Need-idea means you have not planned the next move

## Planning docs practice
- Pick three holes from a tiny cover sketch
- Write one next-test idea per hole in a table
- Star any idea that is only “run more random” and rewrite it to name the bin or corner you

---

## Pitfalls to watch
- Do not mark closed without evidence
- Do not paste one generic idea onto every hole
- Do not ignore mismatch between idea and hole
- And do not confuse board ready with silicon sign-off
