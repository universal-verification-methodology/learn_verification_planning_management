# Verification metrics

Plans need numbers that leadership and engineers share

---

## Bars that matter
- Pass rate below the bar means instability, you cannot trust green stories
- Coverage percent below goal means unfinished plan risk
- Bug escapes above zero at a sev-one bar usually stop the gate
- Levels good, warn, and bad are how you talk without drowning in raw dumps

## Planning docs practice
- Write three metric names with a numeric bar each for a fictional milestone
- Mark today’s sample good, warn, or bad
- If any is bad, write the first action you would take this week

---

## Pitfalls to watch
- Do not call the program healthy because CI ran once
- Do not ignore escape counts when coverage is high
- Do not change bars silently mid-milestone
- And do not track twenty vanity metrics, three honest bars beat a dashboard museum
